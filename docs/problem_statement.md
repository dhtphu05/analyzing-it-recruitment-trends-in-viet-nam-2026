# Problem Statement

## De tai

Phan tich va du bao xu huong tuyen dung IT tai Viet Nam.

## Muc tieu bai toan

Su dung du lieu tuyen dung crawl tu ITViec, TopCV va TopDev de:

- Phan tich xu huong nhu cau ky nang, cap bac, dia diem va loai hinh cong ty.
- Khao sat kha nang du bao muc luong trung binh dua tren dac trung cong viec.

## Dinh nghia bai toan hoc may

- Loai bai toan: Regression
- Bien muc tieu: `salary_avg`
- Don vi muc luong: uu tien quy doi ve VND/thang, co the bo sung USD neu can doi soat.

## Dac trung dau vao

- Tech stack
- Level
- Experience years
- Location
- Company type
- Remote option
- Skills extracted from job description

## Tieu chi toi thieu cua dataset

- So mau > 1000
- It nhat 5 truong thong tin cot loi tren moi tin tuyen dung
- Du lieu co nguon goc tu qua trinh crawl cua nhom

## Cau hoi phan tich goi y

- Cong nghe nao duoc tuyen dung nhieu nhat?
- Muc luong thay doi the nao theo cap bac va kinh nghiem?
- Ha Noi, TP.HCM, Da Nang va Remote co khac biet gi?
- Cum ky nang nao thuong xuat hien cung nhau?
- Co the du bao `salary_avg` voi do chinh xac chap nhan duoc khong?
